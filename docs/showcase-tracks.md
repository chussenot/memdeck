# Showcase Tracks

## dark_moroder.abc

- Inspiration: Giorgio Moroder disco propulsion with dark synth tone
- Instruments: noise kick, pulse bass, pulse arp, triangle lead pad
- Sequencing tricks: call/response bass movement and late lead entry
- FX: punchy bus 0 sidechain, wider delayed bus 1 for melodic space
- Groove: swung 16ths with persistent four-on-the-floor anchor

## perturbator_loop.abc

- Inspiration: modern dark synthwave loop writing
- Instruments: aggressive pulse bass, noise hats, soft triangle pad, pulse lead
- Sequencing tricks: repeating bass ostinato with phrase-end inversions
- FX: bus split keeps bass focused and lead/pad atmospheric
- Groove: urgent BPM with tight hats and short-note lead stabs

## carpenter_drive.abc

- Inspiration: horror-synth pulse and minimal melodic dread
- Instruments: pulse bass, long triangle pad, restrained pulse motif, noise kick

- FX: moderate drive and longer delayed motif tail
- Groove: slower, heavy quarter-note chassis with bridge lift
